The ordering is very subjective, and some items I've listed first purely because I have more interest in them or because they are a stepping stone to something else I'm interested in.

    Key¶

    I understand it enough to fully enjoy it
    I understand on my own, but feel I might be missing hidden meanings
    I get the general idea with little help - Comprehensible input or i+1
    Starting to understand but looking up a lot of words and grammar
    Looking up everything
    Relaxed and enjoyed it, no pressure to look up words or phrases
